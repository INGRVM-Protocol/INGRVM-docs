## **The Intelligence Monopoly is Over.**
Today, we are proud to announce the official launch of the **Calyx Mesh**. 

Calyx is not just an app—it is a distributed neuromorphic nervous system. While traditional AI models require massive data centers and centralized gatekeepers, Calyx runs on the hardware you already own. From your smartphone to your desktop, the mesh shards intelligence across local networks to build something greater than the sum of its parts.

---

### **Pillars of the Mesh**
1.  **Distributed Intelligence (DAP):** Our "Distributed Authority Protocol" ensures no single node owns the brain. Layers are sharded, verified, and propagated across peers in real-time.
2.  **Neuromorphic Efficiency:** Using 1-bit quantization and Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs), we have achieved a **32x reduction** in memory usage, allowing production-grade inference on edge hardware like the Pixel 8.
3.  **Proof-of-Inference (PoI):** A verifiable economy where nodes are rewarded in **$SYN** for performing useful work. Cheating is mathematically impossible; contribution is economically incentivized.
4.  **The Marketplace:** A community-built library of "Neural Seeds"—skills that can be downloaded, merged, and evolved by any node operator.

### **How to Join the Neural Fabric**
1.  **Download the SDK:** Install the Calyx Python SDK to begin programmatically interacting with the mesh.
2.  **Initialize your Node:** Run the `onboarding_wizard.html` to find your role:
    - **Master Nodes:** Orchestrate the mesh and secure the ledger.
    - **Relay Nodes:** Bridge networks and shard heavy layers.
    - **Edge Nodes:** Process inputs and provide real-world data.
3.  **Earn $SYN:** Contribute your hardware's idle capacity to process inference spikes and earn rewards.
